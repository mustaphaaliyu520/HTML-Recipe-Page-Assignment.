Classic Margherita Pizza

A simple yet delicious Italian Classic, with fresh basil, Mozzarella, and a crispy crust.

Prep Time:20-30 mins | Cook Time: 7-10 mins | Servings:20 | Difficlty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 250g of Pizza dough.
  • 1/2 cups of san marzano tomato sauce.
  • Fresh basil leaves.
  • 1 table spoon of extra virgin olive oil.
  • A pinch of salt.

Instructions.

  1. Preheat your oven to 250°C (or as high as it goes).
  2. Roll out the dough on a lightly floured surface until thin.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly, leaving a small border for the crust.
  4. Tear the Mozzarella into tiny chunks, and scatter them into the sauce.
  5. Bake for 10 - 15 mins until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  6. Remove from oven and garnish with fresh basil and a drizzle of oil.

Chef's Tips

Pro Tips: Let the dough sit at room temperature for 30 minutes before rolling it out to make it more elastic | Don't Over-sauce: Too much sauce makes the crust soggy; keep it thin | Dry the Mozzarella: Pat fresh mozzarella dry with paper towels to prevent excess water on top. | Basil Timing: Add basil before baking for a cooked flavor, or immediately after for a fresh, aromatic taste. | High Heat: A hotter oven (550°F) results in a better, crispier crust.
